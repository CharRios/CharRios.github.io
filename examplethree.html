Combining & Mask

For this lab, I needed to choose two photos to mask and combine. I needed to add a filter to the edges to make the final image look as smooth as possible.

I combined a photo of my senior dachshund, Widget, taking a nap on a blanket, and an image of a Whataburger french fry pool float. I used the Quick Selection tool to select Widget. Because he had long hair, I needed to deselect several areas around his edges because they were incorrectly chosen by the Quick Selection tool. I then used the Refine Edge Brush tool to clean up the edges of his hair and the Global Refinements tool, so it looked more natural. I added a new layer to the pool float with the Layer Mask tool. I pasted the new layer of Widget with the blanket and floor removed. I then used the Transform tool to resize and reposition him on the float. It turned out pretty cute!
