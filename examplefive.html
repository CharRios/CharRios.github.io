Fixes & Corrections

For this lab, I needed to choose a photo to correct the lighting or contrast. I was to use the Levels, Curves, Histogram, Shadows/Highlights, and other correction adjustments to fix it.

I chose to color-correct a picture of the graduation cake I made for my niece. The cake was a combination of a Wall-E and a galaxy theme. The photo was very dark, so the galaxy colors didn't appear. To fix this, I used the Brightness/Contrast tool to turn up the brightness and turn down the contrast. I used the Object Selection tool to select the cake, created a new Adjustment Layer, and adjusted the Hue and Saturation. I then started another Adjustment Layer and changed the Brightness and Contrast. I then used the Spot Healing tool on the Background layer to remove some frosting spots I missed during cake assembly from the cake board. I was super excited about how this came out!
