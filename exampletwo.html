Fixes & Corrections

For this lab, I needed to choose a photo with a color tint that needed fixing. I was to use the Hue/Saturation, Color Balance, and other color adjustment tools in Photoshop to edit our image.

I chose an image I took on US-380 heading west. The area is stunning, filled with trees and hills. I first used the Smart Sharpen tool to edit the image to be more vibrant. I then made a few adjustments with the Color Balance Midtones, Brightness/Contrast, and Curves tools. I used the Quick Selection tool to select just the sky. I then used the Exposure tool to make the clouds in the sky more visible as they were washed out in the original photo. I also used the Hue/Saturation tool on the sky to adjust it a bit extra. I am thrilled with how this photo turned out!
